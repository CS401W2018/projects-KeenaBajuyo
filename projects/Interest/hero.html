My Hero - Lebron James

Interest | Hero

LeBron James, famously known as The King, has been my inspiration in playing basketball. Born on December 30, 1984, in Akron, Ohio, he started playing basketball at age 9 and began playing professionally at age 18. I really admire his playstyle, skills, and especially his clutch moments that have been crucial in helping his team. Growing up, I was able to watch his games through television, including all of his championship games, and I must say that I thoroughly enjoyed watching him because those matches showed why he is the team captain.

Teams
He played for three NBA teams. He started with the Cleveland Cavaliers from 2003-2010, then moved to the Miami Heat from 2010-2014 (4 seasons). He returned to the Cleveland Cavaliers from 2014-2018, wrapping up over 11 seasons with them. His current team, and my favorite, is the Los Angeles Lakers, where he has been playing from 2018 to the current season, tallying 7 seasons.
Statistics & Awards
Owning over four NBA championships through his three NBA teams and earning the Finals MVP in those championships, he also earned four Olympic medals representing the United States of America. This definitely shows his dedication and skill to the game, not only in the United States but around the world. He is also the all-time leading scorer with 41,452 points.
Charity & Foundation
He was not able to earn a degree, but despite this he used his platform and earnings to help the people in need. Over $100 million to education and charity. He is known for his philanthropic efforts, which include the LeBron James Family Foundation and the I PROMISE program. Which is one of the reason why I admire him the most. He often gets criticized whenever he has bad plays, but I don't really mind seeing him loose because to me he is a great person that inspires me to be hardworking, generous and humble.
