My Hero - Lebron James

Background

Lebron James also know as The King has been my inspiration in playing basketball. Born in I admire his playstyle, skills and most especially his clutch moments that have been very crucial in helping his team. A little bit of his background he started playing basketball since at age 9 and started playing professionally at age 18.

Statistics & Awards

Owning over 4 NBA Champions and 4 Olympic Medals definitely shows his dedication and skill to the game not only in the United States, but around the world. He is also the all-time leading scorer with 41,452 points.

Charity & Foundation

He was not able to earn a degree, but despite this he used his platform and earnings to help the people in need. over $100 million to education and charity. He is known for his philanthropic efforts, which include the LeBron James Family Foundation and the I PROMISE program. Which is one of the reason why I admire him the most. He often gets criticized whenever he has bad plays, but I don't really mind seeing him loose because to me he is a great person that inspires me to be hardworking, generous and humble.
