What are special entity characters?

Special entity characters are predefined textual representations in HTML that present reserved characters or symbols without being interpreted as code.

What are phrase tags?

HTML phrase tags are the special elements that provide meaning to certain portions of text on a webpage. They make readability and accessibility easier by showing just what purpose or emphasis the text is to serve within the text it encloses.

What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

OL stands for Order List. It gives a list containing items in order, usually in numbers.

UL stands for Unorder List. This gives a list without any order or sequence, just usually with bullets.
