Keena Bajuyo

Welcome to my profile page. I was born and raised in the beautiful islands of the Philippines. My favorite movie series would have to be the Marvel Cinematic Universe, which includes the famous Avengers movies. I love all the heroes in the movies; they all have unique abilities, personalities, and stories that resonate with me, which is why I can relate to them. However, if I were to choose my favorite heroes, they would be:

Top 5 favorite heroes:

  1. Wanda Maximoff - Scarlet Witch
  2. Tony Stark - Iron Man
  3. Stephen Vincent Strange - Doctor Strange
  4. Carol Danvers - Captain Marvel
  5. Steve Rogers - Captain America

In fact, during my senior year in high school, our graduation photo had two themes: one formal attire and the other a person who inspires you or what you want to become. Although heroes are fictional characters, I still chose to portray my favorite hero because she inspires me to be brave despite the uncertainties, and that is Wanda Maximoff, also known as The Scarlet Witch. My favorite quote, which she said in one of the Avengers movies where she decides to accept and face her fears, touches my heart dearly:

"I can't control their fear, only my own." ♥

- Wanda Maximoff - The Scarlet Witch
