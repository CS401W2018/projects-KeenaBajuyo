Questions

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tag has 100% width. The height is as height as the content. Those can change the size with CSS. (Ex. Div, Header, Nav, Footer, Main, H1-6, P, UL, OL, Li, etc.)

Inline tags have limitations in the width they take up, mostly only engaging the space necessary for their content. They do not start on a new line and can sit next to other inline elements. Examples of inline tags include (span, a, img and em.)

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is the outside (around) box (how much space you want around the box)

Padding is the inside of the box (how much space you want between text and border)

Border is a line around the box.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

Absolute units (px, cm, mm, in, pt, pc.)

Relative units (%, em, rem, vw,vh,vmin,vmax.)

When would you use a figure tag?

We use Figure Tag to group images, diagrams, or other illustrations alongside their caption for finer organization and availability in your HTML document.
