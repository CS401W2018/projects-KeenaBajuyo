Engagement - Structural Tags Questions:

What goes in the header?

Website's name, introduction, and logo.

What goes in the Nav?

Various sets of links that connect with each other to guide you to the different sections of the page.

Can the Nav go in the header?

Yes! The Nav can go in the header since it is usually part of the introduction.

Does it have to go in the header?

No! It is not necessary for the Nav to go just in the header. It can go anywhere you think it would fit best, based on your design and functionality.

Does the Nav contain links to another website?

Yes! It depends if you want to put an external link so that it's easier to direct users to wherever they want to go, but usually, the Nav contains the sections of your page.

What goes in the main?

It consists of the main content of your page, highlighting the purpose and other special features you want to let others know about your page.

What goes in the footer?

The best way to include contact information, legal information, and copyright.
